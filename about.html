Program Overview

The Bachelor of Science in Information Technology is a four year degree program that equips students with the knowledge and practical skills necessary to design, develop, and manage innovative information systems. This program combines technical theory, real-world application, and project-based learning to prepare students for successful careers in the ever-evolving field of technology. With a strong emphasis on programming, networking, and emerging tech trends, the BSIT program trains future IT professionals to meet the demands of both local and global industries.

Core Subjects

  1. Computer Programming (Python, Java, C++)
  2. Database Management Systems
  3. Web and Mobile Application Development
  4. Computer Networks and Communication
  5. Systems Analysis and Design

Skills You Will Gain

  1. Advanced problem-solving and algorithmic thinking
  2. Software and application development
  3. Network configuration and administration
  4. Systems design and documentation
  5. Cybersecurity practices and risk assessment

Advantages

  1. High Industry Demand: BSIT graduates are highly sought-after in a wide range of industries including tech, finance, education, healthcare, and government.
  2. Diverse Career Opportunities: Open doors to roles such as software engineer, IT analyst, network administrator, cybersecurity specialist, web developer, and more.
  3. Practical, Hands-On Training: The program includes internships, real-world projects, and simulations to prepare you for actual IT work environments.
